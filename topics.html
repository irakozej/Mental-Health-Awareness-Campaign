Mental Health Awareness

Mental health is an important topic that affects us all. It's essential to raise awareness and provide support to those in need. This page aims to share information and resources related to mental health and well-being.

Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is just as crucial as physical health. It impacts our thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, influencing our overall quality of life. By understanding and addressing mental health issues, we can improve our well-being and help others do the same.

Resources for Mental Health Support

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline
  • Mental Health Rwanda
  • NAMI - National Alliance on Mental Illness

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health issues, please seek help from a mental health professional or a support organization. You are not alone, and there is help available.
